Mystic Realm RPG

Character Design & Environment Art

A deep dive into the visual development of Mystic Realm, exploring the creation of its ethereal characters and haunting landscapes.

Initial Concepts

The early stages of development focused on establishing the mood and tone of the world. We experimented with various color palettes and lighting scenarios to create a sense of mystery and wonder.

Character Modeling

Translating the 2D concepts into 3D required careful attention to topology and silhouette. The main protagonist went through several iterations to ensure the armor design was both functional and visually striking.

Environment Design

The environments were built using a modular asset kit, allowing for rapid iteration of level layouts. Dynamic lighting and particle effects were added to bring the static meshes to life.

About Me

Game Artist & Creative Professional

My Journey

I'm Juliette, a French-Greek Game Artist currently in my final year of a Master's degree in Game Art at IIM Paris.

I specialize in 3D character and creature art, with a strong interest in bringing believable personalities to life through shape, expression, and stylization. I enjoy working across the entire character pipeline, from concept interpretation to modeling, retopology, texturing, and game engine integration.

Throughout my studies, I have contributed to various game projects (couch games, adventure games, boss fights, VR) developed in Unity and Unreal Engine, which helped me build a solid production-oriented workflow and strong teamwork skills.

Alongside my studies, I gained professional experience at Sporty Peppers, a video game startup where I handled the full artistic production, and I am currently working at Thales on 2D and 3D serious games for aviation-related e-learning modules.

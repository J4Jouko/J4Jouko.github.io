About Me

3D Character Modeler

Hey, I'm Juliette!

Thanks a lot for stopping by and taking the time to explore my work ^^

I'm a French-Greek Game Art student, currently in my final year of a Master's degree in Game Art at IIM Paris.

I specialize in 3D art, especially characters and creatures. I love sculpting them piece by piece until they start to feel alive~

Over the years, I've worked on many game projects (couch games, adventure games, boss fights, VR), using Unity and Unreal Engine. I enjoy handling a project from concept to in-game integration, exploring the full character pipeline along the way.

I also have professional experience in the industry! I worked at the video game startup Sporty Peppers, where I took care of the entire artistic side of the projects, and I'm currently working at Thales on 2D/3D serious games for aviation-related e-learning modules~

Before game art, I studied geology, but I eventually chose to follow what had always been there... drawing, creating worlds, and making games ! I'm excited to keep learning, creating characters, and sharing this journey with you ;D
